江戸川 コナン

Information

Conan Edogawa is the alias used by Shinichi Kudo in his shrunken form. Shinichi took the appearance of his six or seven-year-old self after being exposed to a prototype poison called APTX 4869, which he had been forced to swallow by two men in black later revealed to be members of the Black Organization. The poison de-aged Shinichi's entire body except for his nervous system and therefore he still has the personality, memories, and incredible deductive ability of his teenage self.
